Sandro Tonali, "l'onnipresente" to Newcastle

Written byRaúl García Santana   Follow @raulgarciaa15
July 26, 2023
Share on Twitter Share on WhatsApp Share on Facebook Share via Email

Sandro Tonali is an Italian defensive midfielder who was signed by Newcastle (from AC Milan) for an amount of 64 million euros. His reading and vision of the game, mobility, and rival marking stand out.


Playing style:

  • Very high opponent marking
  • Initiation of team pressure
  • Freedom in the midfield
  • Intense ball recovery
  • Set pieces

Compare midfielders

Very high opponent marking:

Sandro is a player who marks the opponent with a lot of continuity. Here we can see that he chases the opposite pivot to make it difficult to get the ball out.

Initiation of team pressure:

By providing offensive aid by occupying a non-defensible space, he decides to put pressure on the opposing goalkeeper to cause the miss. We see that it is him who presses and generates the opposite loss.

Freedom of movement:

Not having a fixed position, he usually hangs around any part of the field to benefit the team. In this play, he opens up on the wing and then puts a cross into the area and the striker finishes off. Such mobility and freedom give him the confidence to be just another striker in the box and occupy space. Here we can see that he tries to find the gap between the defense to embed himself between them and try to finish off, assisting his teammate.

Intense ball recovery:

One of Sandro's most representative qualities is his persistence and desire to win the ball back. The rival tries to go out alone but Tonali manages to recover the ball. That intensity is reflected in divided actions or duels in which with the help of the physical and his play with the body he manages to resume the action. On this occasion, his recovery serves to score a goal.

Set pieces:

Thanks to his technique and vision of the game, he is a great set-piece shooter. He executes a perfect thread so that the defense and the goalkeeper hesitate to go to clear the ball; finally, his partner scores. Another great feature in set pieces is its quality in corner kicks. In this play, Tonali executes a well-measured center so that the striker finishes off almost at will with a great turn of the neck.

Relevant data:

He is the under-23 footballer who produced the most chances during the 2022/23 season in Serie A (62). His probability of assist per game is 0.22, and the number of clear chances per game is 3.11.

Sandro Tonali is a footballer who adapts to the team's style of play and doesn't mind going to any central area of the field to receive the ball. An all-rounder anywhere on the pitch.



For being a very qualitative footballer, Sandro has the knack for acquiring a terrific game changer. He is one of the midfielders whose long pass is longer and more precise than the rest, with game changes of about 40 meters.

How does he fit in Newcastle?:

The formation that Eddie Howe could use would be a 4-3-3 with Tonali a little more free to exploit his qualities. His role in this team is to build from below with Guimarães and then take the play to attack.
